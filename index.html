U.S. MIGRATION stats for your county

People began migrating out of Africa and across our planet over 100,000 years ago. Migrations across what is now the United States have taken place over the past 600 generations, and has been intensely studied by archaeologists and anthropologists. Now, thanks to the U.S. Census Bureau, current migration data is being collected in the American Community Survey, and made available to you.
